Empirical Evidence

Reversal Rate by Volume Intensity — 1-Year Sample
E_q Energy Above P₃ — 60-Day Sliding Windows at N=60 (12 layers, correct operating shell)
Volume Trap — Confirmed
The 2× volume line is a hard boundary. Every high-volume drop in the 1-year sample (0 of 4) failed to recover within 5 days. Below 2×, 4 of 6 recovered. This directly supports the cascade mechanism: forced selling at sufficient intensity creates structural correlations that prevent mean reversion.
E_q Layer Shift — Confirmed at Correct Resolution
Initial analysis at N=720 showed no shift — but that was aliasing: mapping 30 divisors onto 7 DFT bins reads the same data 30 times. At the correct operating shell (N=60, 12 layers, P₃ boundary at q=30), energy above P₃ runs at 22.9% ± 6.8% during normal trading, then rises to 39.6% in the pre-cascade period — a 2.5σ shift that preceded the cascade by six weeks. Peak: 50.4% on Dec 18, 2025.
Methodology Note
The operating shell N must match the signal length. Theorem 8.4 says E_q works for any N, but the coprime-frequency mapping requires L ≥ N to avoid bin aliasing. A 7-point signal has 7 DFT bins — you cannot extract 30 independent measurements from 7 numbers. N=60 gives 12 layers with the P₃ boundary; N=720 requires 720+ data points (≈3 years daily, or ≈5 hours of 1-minute bars). This is an instance of the framework's own Principle 1: perception over prediction — use the instrument at the resolution the data supports.

Academic Corroboration
Topological Data Analysis Confirms the Mechanism
Independent confirmation from an entirely different mathematical framework.
Yen, Xia & Cheong (2021) — Taiwan Stock Exchange, March 2020 Crash
What They Found
Before crash: one giant component (β₀ = 1). As crash progresses, internal voids and holes form (β₁, β₂ increase). Then handle-breaking fragments the graph into many simple components (β₀ jumps to 65, then 91).
What They Didn't Have
TDA sees the topology change but can't explain why the giant component formed (leverage building conditional correlations) or who benefits from the fragmentation sequence (pre-positioned puts). The FVLT fills that gap.
Non-Fusion/Fission Processes Identified
Cavitation — voids forming inside the manifold. Rupture — voids breaking through the surface. Puncture — holes forming. These intermediate topological states precede the final fragmentation — potential early warning signals.
The Bridge
Their Betti numbers measure the same phenomenon the FVLT's primorial classification measures. β₀ fragmenting = primorial layer collapsing after the cascade exhausts itself. Two frameworks, same structure, independent derivation.
